About ~

"Rick and Morty" is an Adult Swim animated series created by Justin Roiland and Dan Harmon. The story follows the adventures of the eccentric mad scientist, Rick Sanchez, and his less experienced grandson, Morty Smith, as they explore the universe with a mix of satirical humor and science fiction elements. The show is known for its creativity, clever humor, and approach to complex concepts.


~ Characters

Rick Sanchez

Rick Sanchez is the co-eponymous main character and leading protagonist of the show. He is a genius scientist whose alcoholism and reckless, nihilistic behavior are a source of concern for his daughter's family, as well as the safety of their son, Morty.

Rick's intellect and nihilistic outlook on life have made him a deeply bitter individual, though it has also made him somewhat of a hero due to his willingness to do whatever it takes to keep his family safe, even if it means destroying the world. He is also a firm believer in the concept of determinism, believing that all things, even his own actions, are predetermined and that free will is merely an illusion.

Morty Smith

Mortimer "Morty" Smith Sr. is one of the two eponymous main protagonists in Rick and Morty. He is the grandson of Rick and is often forced to tag along on his various misadventures. He is voiced by Justin Roiland.
